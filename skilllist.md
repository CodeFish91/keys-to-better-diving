# Skilllist
*Initial list*

## Basic skills
- Regulator recovery
- Remove, replace & clear mask + no mask breathing + no mask swim
- Hand signals
- Oral BCD inflation
- Remove and replace scuba kit
- disconnect low pressure hose
- weight removal & replacement
- air management within 20bar
- Set up and shoot DSMB
- Compass navigation

## Equipment skills
- Full assembly and check of equipment
- Weight check at the end of the dive (max 50bar left)

## Enter and exit
- Negative Entry
- Stride entry
- Backward roll
- Remove fins in water
- Remove BCD and weights in water to hand to staff on boat

## Buoyancy & Propulsion skills
- Neutral buoyancy
- No ground touching/no disturbance of the ground
- Flutter kick/modified flutter kick
- Frog kick/modified frog kick
- Back kick
- Helicopter turn

## Dry suit skills
- Emergency Roll
- Inflator Hose disconnect
### Advanced:
- Feet up float and recovery

## Sidemount skills
- Tired Diver Tow (from 5m to surface)
- Right tank drop & tank grab (if no stage)
- Out of air drill & Longhose stow
- Stage handling - Stage drop and grab
- Stage handling - Stage rotation
- Valve Feathering
- Pouch Access & Pouch Store

## Rescue skills
- Tired diver tow
- controlled emergency swimming ascent (CESA)
- Alternate air source use

## CCR
- Bailout
- Dilluent flush
- Loop water purge (if the CCR allows it)

## Cave Diving
 - blind line sweep
 - blind exit
